Key bindings:

(The following bindings are case sensitive)
(E.g. Key 'W' = 'shift' + 'w')

W : Moves Olaf forward
S : Moves Olaf backwards
A : Moves Olaf to his left
D : Moves Olaf to his right

a : Rotates Olaf 5 degrees along the positive Y-axis
d : Rotates Olaf 5 degrees along the negative Y-axis

W + D : Turn clockwise
W + A : Turn counter-clockwise

Left arrow : Rotates world left
Right arrow : Rotates world right
Up arrow : Rotates world up
Down arrow : Rotates world down

Holding right mouse button and mouse movement in x : Pans the camera
Holding middle mouse button (or key 'v') and mouse movement in y : Tilts the camera
Holding the left mouse button and mouse movment in y : Zooms in/out the camera by changing FOV

Key 'HOME' or key 'h' : Resest the world orientation and camera fov

Spacebar : Randomly positions Olaf on the grid
r : Resests Olaf's position, rotation and scale 

b : Toggle shadows
x : Toggle textures

=============== Custom limb movements =====================

	1 : Toggle between custom limb movements

	2 : Move left arm in positive lateral direction
shift + 2 : Move left arm in positive vertical direction
	3 : Move left arm in negative lateral direction
shift + 3 : Move left arm in negative vertical direction

	4 : Move right arm in positive lateral direction
shift + 4 : Move right arm in positive vertical direction
	5 : Move right arm in negative lateral direction
shift + 5 : Move right arm in negative vertical direction

	6 : Move left foot in negative lateral direction
shift + 6 : Move left foot in negative vertical direction
	7 : Move left foot in positive lateral direction
shift + 7 : Move left foot in positive vertical direction

	8 : Move right foot in positive lateral direction
shift + 8 : Move right foot in positive vertical direction
	9 : Move right foot in negative lateral direction
shift + 9 : Move right foot in negative vertical direction
